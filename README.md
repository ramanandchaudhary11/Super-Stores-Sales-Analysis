1. Project Title

Super Store Sales Dashboard ‚Äì Power BI Project

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze and visualize sales performance, customer behavior, and profitability across different regions for a retail superstore.

2. Project Overview

The Super Store Sales Dashboard provides a complete view of business performance through dynamic visuals and KPIs.
It helps stakeholders track sales, profit, orders, and shipping performance across different customer segments, categories, and regions.
The goal is to turn raw transactional data into actionable insights for better strategic and operational decision-making.

4. Data Source

Dataset used: Superstore Sales Dataset (public sample data available on Kaggle).

Contains around 10,000 records with attributes such as Order ID, Customer Segment, Category, Sub-Category, Region, Payment Mode, Sales, Profit, Ship Date,etc.

Data was cleaned and modeled in Power Query before visualization.

5. Business Problem

The management team needed a clear understanding of:

Which products, regions, and customer segments generate the most profit.

How sales and profits fluctuate over time.

Which shipping and payment methods are most efficient and preferred.

Where business opportunities and inefficiencies exist.

6. Goal of the Dashboard

To create a single, interactive Power BI report that:

Tracks key performance metrics (Sales, Profit, Orders, Shipping Days).

Helps management identify profitable categories and high-performing regions.

Highlights trends, patterns, and seasonal growth in sales and profit.

Supports data-driven decision-making through visual analytics.

7. Dashboard Features

Key KPIs: Total Orders ‚Äì 22K, Sales ‚Äì 1.57M, Profit ‚Äì 175K, Average Ship Days ‚Äì 4.

Sales by Payment Mode: COD (43%) is the most preferred, followed by Online (35%) and Cards (22%).

Sales by Segment: Consumer segment leads with 48% of total sales, followed by Corporate (33%) and Home Office (19%).

Regional Analysis: West region contributes the highest (33%) while South is the lowest (16%).

Sales by Category: Office Supplies generate the most sales (644K), followed by Technology (471K) and Furniture (452K).

Sales by Sub-Category: Phones (197K) and Chairs (182K) are the top-selling items.

Sales by Ship Mode: Standard shipping dominates with 330K orders, followed by Second Class and First Class.

Monthly Trends: Clear growth in sales and profit during the last quarter (October‚ÄìDecember).

Geographic Analysis: Interactive map shows sales and profit distribution across U.S. states.

8. Key Insights

Consumer segment contributes nearly half of total sales.

West region leads in both sales and profitability.

Office Supplies and Technology categories are the main revenue drivers.

COD is the most preferred payment mode among customers.

October to December show the highest seasonal demand and profit margins.

Shipping efficiency is good with an average of 4 days per order.

Phones, Chairs, and Binders are the top-performing subcategories.

Profitability aligns closely with Technology and Office Supplies.

Standard shipping covers the majority of orders, indicating a preference for cost-effective delivery.

Regional performance insights can guide future marketing and logistics planning.

9. Business Impact

Helped identify high-profit product categories and regions.

Improved marketing strategy by focusing on the most profitable customer segment.

Supported better inventory and supply chain management through regional insights.

Enabled faster and more informed business decisions through visual reporting.
